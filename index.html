Vision

My graduate training is in high-fidelity multidisciplinary design optimization, which involves developing accurate and efficient computational models of physical systems, customizing them to interface with a gradient-based optimizer, and running paral- lelized programs on HPC clusters. I have experience developing optimization software and architecting system models in Cameo at Northrop Grumman. I am interested in opportunities to develop computational models of physical processes and harness these models to inform design decisions.
